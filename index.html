Ama, LA

Growing up in South Louisiana i was afforded the oppurtunity to see a lot of the wild life as well as the city of New Orleans. Going fishing during the day and ending up in New Orleans for dinner and a night out was very common and a way of life down south.

New Orleans, LA

Going to the French Quarter as a kid and experiencing the culture, life, art and food really opened my mind. I was thrown into a very loving and accepting culture that the south offers and in turn incoporated that mentality into my lifestyle. Even after joining the Marine Corps my southern roots and traditions remained and i was able use that to build some of the best friendships anyone could ask for.

Al Anbar Province, Iraq

Leaving New Orleans after joining the Marine Corps in January 2013 was bitter sweet. Everything and everyone i knew was left behind and i had to restart. I finished boot camp and trade school and was sent to my first unit, V2/7. In September of 2014 i was then deployed to Iraq 19 months after joining. It was a whirlwind of emotions but it was a great experience that i endured with some amazing people. The days were long, nights were short and times were very memorable. If i had to go back and do it all over again I'd probably do it twice.

Washington D.C.

After doing two tours overseas it was time to move onto the next chapter. I re-enlisted in 2017 and received orders to Marine Corps Installations Command at the Pentagon. If you would've told me 5 years ago the Marine Corps would take me to the Pentagon I would've laughed, yet here i am. Coming here allowed me to really hone in on the skills i wasn't utilizing while being with the infantry. I've been afforded many oppurtunities to work alongside very knowledgable Cyber Security personnel as well as Network Administrators.

My Story

Well this has been my journey with more to add later on down the road. I hope to look back on this project to show where i started and to stay humble. There will always be someone smarter but it's up to you to learn from that person and make yourself better.
